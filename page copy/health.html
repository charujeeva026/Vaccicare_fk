Health Guidelines

Healthy Tips

Learn about various healthy practices for maintaining a balanced lifestyle.

Healthy Eating

  • Incorporate more fruits and vegetables
  • Choose whole grains
  • Limit processed foods and added sugar
  • Stay hydrated — drink water throughout the day

Exercise & Meditation

  • Engage in regular physical activity (30 min/day)
  • Practice simple yoga or breathing exercises
  • Take short daily walks — improve mood & circulation
  • Prioritize 7–9 hours of restful sleep

Daily Habits

  • Wash hands regularly and maintain hygiene
  • Limit screen time before bed
  • Keep small, frequent healthy meals if pregnant

Mental Well-being

  • Practice gratitude or journaling
  • Connect with friends or support groups
  • Take short breathing breaks during the day
