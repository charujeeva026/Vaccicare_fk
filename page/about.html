About VacciCare

VacciCare is a user-friendly platform designed to help pregnant women and parents manage their vaccination schedules and stay informed about health guidelines.

Our Mission

To make vaccination tracking easier, improve health awareness, and connect users with hospitals and doctors quickly and efficiently.

Our Vision

To create a healthy, informed community using technology that supports both mothers and children at every stage of care.
